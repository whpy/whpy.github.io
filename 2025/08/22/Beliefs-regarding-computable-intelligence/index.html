Beliefs_regarding_computable_intelligence

我们应当相信，基于确定性的计算机架构所设计的模拟智能必定是可被完全解析的。基于此，我们应当坚信，更好的模拟智能必定来源于可计算理论中的某些根本性的特性。因此对于下一个时代的模拟智能，我们应当探索更底层的，独属于可计算问题的一些特性，而非继续从仿生学的角度去思考和取材。

地球上速度最快的物体不是猎豹和虫子，而是赛车和火箭。

We should hold the belief that simulated intelligence designed on deterministic computer architectures must be fully analyzed. Based on this, we ought to firmly believe that more advanced simulated intelligence inevitably arise from certain fundamental properties within computable theory. Therefore, for the next-gen simulated intelligence, we should explore deeper, intrinsic characteristics unique to computatble problems, rather than continue approaching the issue from a biomimetic perspective.

The fastest object on Earth are not cheetahs or insects, but race cars and rockets.
